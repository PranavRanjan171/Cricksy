About
BUILT BY A
CRICKET FAN,
FOR THE STREETS

Criksy was born out of frustration. Every time we played in the society, someone miscounted, someone forgot who bowled — and the apps that existed required accounts, subscriptions, or five people with different phones.

So I built Criksy: one phone, one scorer, zero friction. Designed for real India — the terrace matches, the parking lot fours, the last-ball finish the whole colony talks about.
